Our Ongoing Projects

The InAmigos Foundation runs six core social initiatives focused on education, hunger relief, women's empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and youth employability.

Project SEVA

Project SEVA is InAmigos Foundation's flagship initiative dedicated to combating hunger, poverty, and clothing deprivation. Through food distribution drives, meal-kit programs, and clothing donation campaigns, the project supports underprivileged families, daily wage workers, orphanages, and homeless individuals. The initiative also promotes sustainable food redistribution by collecting surplus food from events and organizations. Since its launch, Project SEVA has impacted thousands of lives across multiple cities, ensuring that vulnerable communities receive not only essential resources but also dignity, care, and hope for a better future.

Project Bachpanshala

Project Bachpanshala focuses on providing quality educational opportunities to underprivileged children who lack access to proper schooling and learning resources. The initiative supports children through educational materials, digital literacy programs, life-skills training, and awareness sessions. InAmigos Foundation believes that education is a fundamental right and a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. By nurturing curiosity, confidence, and critical thinking, Bachpanshala empowers children to dream bigger, pursue their goals, and become responsible contributors to society.

Project Vikas

Project Vikas is a youth empowerment initiative designed to bridge the gap between education and employability. Through internship programs, workshops, and skill-development opportunities, participants gain practical experience in areassuch as digital marketing, content writing, graphic design,public relations, research, and event management. With thousands of interns onboarded across India, the initiative helps students build professional skills, improve career readiness, and develop leadership qualities. Project Vikas transforms potential into opportunity by preparing young individuals for successful careers and meaningful social impact.

Project Udaan

Project Udaan is dedicated to empowering women through education, skill development, financial independence, and awareness programs. The initiative supports women by providing training in entrepreneurship, digital literacy, tailoring, handicrafts, and leadership development. It also conducts workshops on women's rights, health, and menstrual hygiene awareness. By collaborating with self-help groups and local communities, Project Udaan creates opportunities for women to become self-reliant and confident. The project has helped hundreds of women improve their livelihoods, strengthen their families, and become active leaders within their communities.

Project Jeev

Project Jeev promotes compassion and animal welfare by focusing on the rescue, feeding, protection, and care of stray animals. Through a dedicated network of volunteers, the initiative works to ensure that neglected animals receive food, medical attention, and safe living conditions. Beyond direct care, Project Jeev raises awareness about responsible animal welfare and encourages communities to coexist peacefully with stray animals. The project contributes to healthier environments, stronger community participation, and a culture of empathy where every life is valued and protected.

Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti is an environmental conservation initiative that promotes sustainability through tree plantation drives, ecological awareness campaigns, and support for eco-friendly practices. The project encourages individuals and communities to take active steps toward protecting natural resources and reducing environmental degradation. Through large-scale plantation activities and environmental education programs, Prakriti inspires citizens to contribute to a greener and healthier future. By nurturing environmental responsibility today, the initiative aims to create lasting positive change for future generations.
