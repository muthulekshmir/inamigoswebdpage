About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organization committed to community development and social empowerment. Registered under Section 8 and certified under 80G, 12A, CSR-1, and ISO 9001:2015, the foundation works to create meaningful and sustainable social change.

We focus on improving lives through education, environmental conservation, women empowerment, animal welfare, and community support. Through innovative projects and volunteer-driven initiatives, we aim to build a more inclusive and equitable society.
